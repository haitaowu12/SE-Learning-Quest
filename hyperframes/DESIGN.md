# SE Learning Quest Motion Design

## Style Prompt

Polished professional learning simulator with clean systems diagrams, coffee-service warmth, rail-domain discipline, and evidence-thread motion. The motion language should feel like a trace being built from decision to artifact to evidence, not like entertainment-first game animation.

## Colors

- `#f7f8f4` page canvas
- `#17211c` primary text and diagram ink
- `#183d31` deep systems green
- `#b8642a` coffee amber accent
- `#377b83` proof blue accent
- `#e8b55a` measured-highlight accent

## Typography

- Headings: `Syne`, fallback sans-serif
- Body and labels: `Space Grotesk`, fallback sans-serif

## Motion Rules

- Animate trace paths, state transitions, and evidence reveals.
- Keep motion slow enough for learners to read labels.
- Provide static SVG/poster fallbacks in the app.
- Respect reduced motion in the webapp.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use private standards excerpts in visual assets.
- Do not create classroom/facilitator framing.
- Do not use decorative motion that obscures the learning relationship.
- Do not rely on video playback for required learner content.
